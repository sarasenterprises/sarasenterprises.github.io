About Us

Saras Enterprises is a Pune, India based company, established in 1993. We are a team of talented engineers experienced in machining parts with tolerances within 10 microns. We are catered for producing complex product machining as per your requirements and within all the extreme tolerances.

  • 30 years of professional experience in CNC machining of extremely critical components.
  • Guaranteed precise machining of all variety of materials with high productivity.
  • Long term reliability for customers.
  • High expertise and dedicated team for new development.
  • End to End service from drawing to delivery.
  • Direct export service available for international customers.

Arvind Habade

CEO

The founder aka the soul of SARAS Enterprises, established SARAS Enterprises with a motive to serve the best CNC machined products in the market. Starting from the scratch that means learning the basics of machining principles and use of Lathe machines, he has mastered the art of churning precision components from possibly all types of materials along with a high productivity. Since the early 2000’s CNC machining has been a trending manufacturing technology in India and today almost every other industry needs CNC machining.
